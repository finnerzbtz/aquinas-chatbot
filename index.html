Welcome to Aquinas Chatbot

This is a demonstration of the Aquinas Chatbot. You should see a chat button in the bottom right corner of the page.

Features

  • Click the chat button to open the chat interface
  • Type your message and press Enter or click Send
  • Click outside the chat window or the X button to close it

About

The Aquinas Chatbot is designed to provide assistance and information. Click the chat button to start a conversation!
